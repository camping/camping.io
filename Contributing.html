You can contribute to Camping in several ways. For starters:

  • check through the issues and see what you can do
  • join the mailing list and help out answering questions
  • be available on IRC (#camping @ irc.freenode.net) when you can
  • write something useful on deployment for your setup (for example)
  • try something new and let the community know how it went
  • send your Camping app/site to the mailing list so it can be included
  • help us finish the website

You don't have to be a Ruby expert, but that helps too...
